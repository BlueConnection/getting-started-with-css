Trips that changed my life

Feb 7 to Feb 19, 2024

Philippines

Embarking on a recent journey to the Philippines, I found myself immersed in a tapestry of experiences that unfolded across bustling metropolises and serene retreats alike. From the lively streets of Metro Manila to the modern allure of BGC, and the historic charm of Intramuros, each destination offered a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Filipino culture. Venturing further, the tranquil beauty of Baguio, the scenic vistas of Tagaytay, and the warm hospitality of Urdaneta left an indelible mark on my heart. And of course, who could forget the sun-drenched shores of Boracay, where I reveled in the beauty of its beaches? Amidst my adventures, the culinary delights of the Philippines tantalized my taste buds, from savory delicacies to the refreshing sweetness of fresh coconut water. The grandeur of the malls, each with its unique charm, captivated my senses, offering a glimpse into the vibrancy of Filipino urban life. As a first-time visitor to a developing country, this journey was not just a series of destinations but a profound awakening to the privilege I have back home, fostering a deep sense of gratitude and appreciation for the wonders of the world.

Off the top of my head memories:

  • Riding a stranger's car to a laundry place.
  • Flagging an e-trike in the rain at Boracay's D'mall.
  • Sitting on a stool while waiting for my turn at Mercury Drug.
  • Laughing hysterically in the basement of an AirBnb due to exhaustion and heat.

Feb 2 to Feb 7, 2024

Tokyo

In the span of just four days, my journey to Japan with my girlfriend and her family was nothing short of magical. Our home base near Oshiage Station, with the iconic SKYTREE towering nearby, provided the perfect launchpad for our adventures. After settling in, we wasted no time indulging in authentic sushi at a nearby Kura, savoring the flavors of Japan from the moment we arrived. Our exploration took us to the historic streets of Asakusa, where we immersed ourselves in the rich tapestry of Japanese culture, admiring the Senso-ji Temple and browsing through bustling markets. A visit to Tokyo DisneySea added an extra sprinkle of excitement to our trip, where we reveled in the enchanting world of Disney magic. The bustling energy of Shibuya captivated us as we crossed the famous scramble intersection and explored its vibrant streets. And of course, no trip to Tokyo would be complete without a stroll down Takeshita Street, where we indulged in quirky fashion finds and delectable street food delights. Amidst our adventures, the unexpected beauty of snowfall in Tokyo added a surreal touch to our experience, transforming the city into a winter wonderland. Though our time in Japan was brief, the memories we made will last a lifetime, filled with laughter, discovery, and the warmth of shared experiences.

Off the top of my head memories:

  • Snow, slush, and rain in Tokyo.
  • The walk to get out of Oshiage Station (SKYTREE).
  • Drinking Iced Matcha beside Ueno Station.
  • The walk from Takeshita Street to Shibuya.
