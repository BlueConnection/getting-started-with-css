Trips that changed my life

Latest trip

Philippines

Embarking on a recent journey to the Philippines, I found myself immersed in a tapestry of experiences that unfolded across bustling metropolises and serene retreats alike. From the lively streets of Metro Manila to the modern allure of BGC, and the historic charm of Intramuros, each destination offered a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Filipino culture. Venturing further, the tranquil beauty of Baguio, the scenic vistas of Tagaytay, and the warm hospitality of Urdaneta left an indelible mark on my heart. And of course, who could forget the sun-drenched shores of Boracay, where I reveled in the beauty of its beaches? Amidst my adventures, the culinary delights of the Philippines tantalized my taste buds, from savory delicacies to the refreshing sweetness of fresh coconut water. The grandeur of the malls, each with its unique charm, captivated my senses, offering a glimpse into the vibrancy of Filipino urban life. As a first-time visitor to a developing country, this journey was not just a series of destinations but a profound awakening to the privilege I have back home, fostering a deep sense of gratitude and appreciation for the wonders of the world.

Off the top of my head memories:

  • Riding a stranger's car to a laundry place
  • Flagging an e-trike in the rain at Boracay's D'mall
  • Sitting on a stool while waiting for my turn at Mercury Drug
